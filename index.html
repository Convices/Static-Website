Autonomous Cars

With the advent of latest technologies, Autonomous cars or self driving cars are no more a dream but has become concrete reality. Autonomous cars stand for a new way forward in automobile industry. An Autonomous car is a vehicle that can guide itself without human conduction and use various kinds of technologies including GPS sensing knowledge to help with navigation and other sensors to avoid collision. It also use augmented reality, where a vehicle displays information to drivers in new and innovative ways. Autonomous cars, in order to apprehend their surroundings are mainly equipped with a variety of sensors such as radar, computer vision, lidar, sonar, odometry and inertial measurement units. Our team have been working on different technological aspects that are needed to build the required software for Autonomous cars. The main benefit of Autonomous cars is reducing the number of traffic collisions with the help of technologies like computer vision, lidar, radar, odometry and GPS. Also Autonomous cars also help people who can't drive whether elderly, blind or disabled.

Mixed Reality

Mixed reality is the use of both virtual (VR) and augmented (AR) reality technologies to create an environment where physical and virtual objects can exist and interact in real-time. This ability to interact with both physical and virtual objects gives mixed reality technologies a huge number of potential applications. We believe Mixed Reality can bring better solutions to your business with more visibility and interest towards your products and services. As an independent concept, mixed reality combines the best of both virtual reality and augmented reality. Blockchain

Blockchain being the emerging technology can disrupt a lot of fields especially fintech. The theoretical immutability feature of the blockchain is one that endears people to it greatly. The essential power of blockchain technology is its ability to distribute information. Blockchain with its distributed ledger technology is rapidly trending as the perfect alternative to manage millions and billions of network nodes and transactions. Apart from financial sector Blockchain helps organizations achieve greater visibility across the supply chain and enhance traceability of products across the value chain, making it the perfect addition to enterprise systems and processes. Our prime focus is to build core blockchain solutions for global market. In particular we aim on building scalable and reliable peer to peer network, ICO platform, Cryptocurrency wallet, Computing platforms and distributed storage based on customer requirement.
